From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

Founded in 2015 by a small group of passionate developers, Stratek Softwares began as a modest startup with big dreams. What started as a team of three working from a shared workspace has grown into a thriving company with over 50 talented professionals.

Our journey hasn't been without challenges, but each obstacle has strengthened our resolve and sharpened our expertise. Today, we're proud to have delivered over 200 successful projects to clients across various industries, from ambitious startups to established enterprises.

Despite our growth, we've maintained our startup spirit - agile, innovative, and deeply committed to our clients' success. We believe technology should be an enabler, not a barrier, and this philosophy guides everything we do.
